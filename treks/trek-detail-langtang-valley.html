Langtang Valley Trek

Hiking in the Heart of the Nepalese Himalayas

The Langtang Valley Trek is a spectacular and accessible trekking route located north of Kathmandu, within the Langtang National Park. Less crowded than the Annapurna or Everest regions, this trek offers an authentic Himalayan experience, combining pristine nature with rich local culture.

The trail passes through beautiful pine, bamboo, and rhododendron forests, traditional Tamang villages, and wide alpine landscapes dominated by Langtang Lirung (7,227 m), Ganesh Himal, and surrounding peaks. Along the way, trekkers discover Buddhist monasteries, high-altitude pastures, and a deeply rooted mountain lifestyle.

With well-balanced daily stages and comfortable mountain lodges, this trek is suitable for both beginners and experienced hikers. It perfectly combines adventure, gradual acclimatization, cultural immersion, and breathtaking scenery, offering an unforgettable journey into the heart of the Nepalese Himalayas.

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 1

Kathmandu → Syabrubesi (1,460 m)

Early morning departure by vehicle to Syabrubesi, the gateway to the Langtang Valley. The scenic drive passes through hills, terraced fields, rivers, and traditional villages. Overnight in a lodge.

Drive Lodge
Day 2

Syabrubesi → Lama Hotel (2,470 m)

The trek begins along the Langtang Khola River through subtropical forests and bamboo groves. Passing small villages, the trail gradually ascends to Lama Hotel. Overnight in a lodge.

~11 km +1,010 m Lodge
Day 3

Lama Hotel → Langtang Village (3,430 m)

The trail climbs through dense rhododendron and pine forests before opening into a wide alpine valley. Arrival at Langtang Village, rebuilt after the 2015 earthquake. Overnight in a lodge.

~14 km +960 m Lodge
Day 4

Langtang Village → Kyanjin Gompa (3,870 m)

A gentle ascent through yak pastures and high-altitude villages leads to Kyanjin Gompa, the spiritual center of the region and an ideal place for acclimatization. Overnight in a lodge.

~7 km +440 m Lodge
Day 5

Acclimatization Day at Kyanjin Gompa

Acclimatization hike with an optional ascent of Kyanjin Ri or Tserko Ri, offering spectacular panoramic views of Langtang Lirung and surrounding peaks. Overnight in a lodge.

~7 km +/- 700 to 1,200 m Lodge
Day 6

Kyanjin Gompa → Lama Hotel

Descent through the Langtang Valley, passing forests and villages on the way back to Lama Hotel. Overnight in a lodge.

~21 km -1,400 m Lodge
Day 7

Lama Hotel → Syabrubesi

Final trekking day descending along the Langtang Khola River back to Syabrubesi. Overnight in a lodge.

~11 km -1,010 m Lodge
Day 8

Syabrubesi → Kathmandu

Return by vehicle to Kathmandu. End of the trek.

Drive Hotel or Own arrangement
