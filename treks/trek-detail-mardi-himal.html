Mardi Himal Trek

An Authentic Adventure in the Heart of the Nepalese Himalayas

The Mardi Himal Trek is one of the most beautiful short treks in Nepal, located in the renowned Annapurna region. Still largely untouched by mass tourism, this trek offers an authentic experience combining pristine nature, spectacular mountain scenery, and cultural immersion.

Ideal for trekking and adventure enthusiasts, the Mardi Himal Trek provides outstanding views of Machapuchare (Fishtail), Annapurna South, and Hiunchuli.

Duration 6 Days
Level Moderate
Group 2 - 10 Pers.
Max Alt. 4,500 m

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 1

Drive to Kande (1,770 m) & Trek to Deurali (2,100 m)

From Pokhara, drive to Kande, the starting point of the trek. The hike begins with a gradual ascent through rhododendron forests and Gurung villages to Deurali. Overnight in a lodge.

~6 km +550 m Lodge
Day 2

Deurali to Forest Camp (2,520 m)

The trail ascends gently through dense, humid forest, often shrouded in mist, creating a peaceful and wild atmosphere. This day allows for gentle acclimatization before reaching Forest Camp. Overnight in a lodge.

~7 km +420 m Lodge
Day 3

Forest Camp to High Camp (3,580 m)

The climb becomes steeper as the trail gradually leaves the forest and reaches the open ridges of Mardi Himal. First panoramic views of Machapuchare (Fishtail) appear along the way. Arrival at High Camp, a spectacular viewpoint facing the Annapurna range. Overnight in a lodge.

~9 km +1,060 m Lodge
Day 4

High Camp - Mardi Base Camp - Low Camp (2,970 m)

Early morning departure to reach Mardi Himal Base Camp, located on a narrow ridge offering exceptional views of Annapurna South, Hiunchuli, and Machapuchare. After time at the viewpoint, descend to Low Camp. Overnight in a lodge.

~12 km +920 m / -1,530 m Lodge
Day 5

Low Camp to Siding (1,750 m)

Descend through bamboo forests and isolated settlements to the village of Siding, still rarely visited by trekkers. This day marks the end of the trekking section. Overnight in a lodge or homestay.

~8 km -1,220 m Homestay
Day 6

Siding to Pokhara

Drive back to Pokhara, passing terraced rice fields and lush valleys. Hotel check-in and free time by Phewa Lake.
