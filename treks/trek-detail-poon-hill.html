Poon Hill Trek

Short Trek with Spectacular Panoramas in Nepal

The Poon Hill Trek is one of the most popular and accessible treks in Nepal, making it ideal for beginners or travelers with limited time. This short trek leads through the picturesque valleys of the Annapurna region, traditional Gurung and Magar villages, and lush rhododendron forests, before reaching Poon Hill (3,210 m)—famous for its breathtaking sunrise views over Annapurna South, Machapuchare (Fishtail), and Dhaulagiri.

Duration 6 Days
Level Easy-Moderate
Group 2 - 12 Pers.
Max Alt. 3,210 m

The route offers an authentic cultural experience with comfortable lodge accommodation and locally prepared meals. Easily completed in 3 to 5 days, the Poon Hill Trek is an unforgettable adventure and a perfect introduction to trekking in the Nepalese Himalayas.

Day by Day Itinerary

Classic Annapurna Foothills Route

Day 1

Drive from Pokhara to Nayapul (1,070 m) & Trek to Ulleri (1,965 m)

Departure by vehicle from Pokhara to Nayapul, then begin the trek on a steep stone staircase trail ascending to Ulleri. Overnight in a lodge.

~8 km ~3 hrs +900 m / -0 m
Day 2

Ulleri to Ghorepani (2,750 m)

The trail continues through magnificent forests of giant rhododendrons to reach Ghorepani. Overnight in a lodge.

~9 km 5-5.5 hrs +800 m / -50 m
Day 3

Poon Hill (3,210 m) Sunrise & Trek to Tadapani (2,630 m)

Early morning hike to Poon Hill to witness a spectacular sunrise with panoramic views of the Annapurna range, Machapuchare, and Dhaulagiri. Afterward, descend and continue trekking to Tadapani. Overnight in a lodge.

~12 km 6-7 hrs +500 m / -650 m
Day 4

Tadapani to Ghandruk (1,940 m)

Walk to Ghandruk, one of the most beautiful Gurung villages in the region. Explore the village and enjoy the mountain views. Overnight in a lodge.

~8 km ~4 hrs +100 m / -800 m
Day 5

Ghandruk to Jhinu Danda (1,750 m)

Descend through bamboo forests and cross a 300-meter suspension bridge to reach Jhinu Danda, where you can relax in the natural hot springs. Overnight in a lodge.

~7 km ~4 hrs +200 m / -450 m
Day 6

Drive from Jhinu Danda to Pokhara

Return by vehicle to Pokhara (approximately 3-4 hours).

Vehicle 3-4 hrs
