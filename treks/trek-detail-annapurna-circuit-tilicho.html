Annapurna Circuit with Tilicho Lake

Trekking in Nepal

The Annapurna Circuit with Tilicho Lake is one of the most iconic treks in Nepal, combining adventure, spectacular Himalayan landscapes, and deep cultural immersion. This unique itinerary crosses the Annapurna mountain range, passes through traditional villages of Manang and the Mustang region, and reaches Tilicho Lake (4,919 m)—one of the highest-altitude lakes in the world.

Trekkers enjoy outstanding panoramic views of Annapurna I, Dhaulagiri, Machapuchare, and Gangapurna, while exploring diverse valleys, high mountain passes such as Thorong La (5,416 m), and authentic Nepalese villages. This trek blends physical challenge, high altitude, and natural beauty, offering comfortable lodges, safe organization, and a complete Annapurna region experience. The Annapurna Circuit with Tilicho Lake is ideal for experienced trekkers seeking an authentic and unforgettable adventure in Nepal.

Duration 12 Days
Level Sustained

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 1

Kathmandu to Besisahar, then Jeep to Dharapani (1,700 m)

Departure from Kathmandu by vehicle to Besisahar, followed by a local jeep ride to Dharapani. Overnight in a lodge.

Lodge
Day 2

Dharapani to Chame (2,670 m)

After a steep ascent to Upper Timang, the trail continues through beautiful pine and oak forests before reaching Chame, with remarkable views of Annapurna and Manaslu. Overnight in a lodge.

~5.5 hours +1,042 m / -364 m Lodge
Day 3

Chame to Pisang (3,200 m)

Cross several Gurung villages as the landscape becomes increasingly arid. Arrival in Pisang. Overnight in a lodge.

~5 hours +774 m / -225 m Lodge
Day 4

Pisang to Manang (3,540 m)

A spectacular day with stunning views of the Annapurna range, passing through the villages of Gyaru and Ngawal before reaching Manang. Overnight in a lodge.

~7 hours +811 m / -622 m Lodge
Day 5

Acclimatization Day in Manang

Acclimatization day with a hike to the Gangapurna Glacier viewpoint and the option to visit Braga village and its beautiful monastery. Overnight in Manang.

Lodge
Day 6

Manang to Tilicho Base Camp (4,150 m) via Khangsar

Depart from Manang and trek via Khangsar to Tilicho Base Camp. Overnight in a lodge.

~7 hours Lodge
Day 7

Tilicho Lake (4,950 m) & Trek to Shree Kharka (3,950 m)

Early morning hike from Tilicho Base Camp to Tilicho Lake, one of the highest lakes in the world (round trip ~4.5 hours). Afterward, continue trekking to Shree Kharka. Overnight in a lodge.

~6.5 hours Lodge
Day 8

Shree Kharka to Yak Kharka (4,040 m)

Trek from Shree Kharka to Yak Kharka through alpine terrain. Overnight in a lodge.

~4.5 hours Lodge
Day 9

Yak Kharka to Thorong Phedi / High Camp (4,450-4,800 m)

Gradual but sometimes steep ascent to Thorong Phedi or High Camp, located at the foot of the Thorong La Pass. Overnight in a lodge.

4-5.5 hours Lodge
Day 10

Thorong La Pass (5,416 m) - Muktinath (3,700 m)

Early morning start to cross Thorong La Pass, the highest point of the trek. After the pass, descend to Muktinath, a sacred pilgrimage site for both Hindus and Buddhists. Overnight in a lodge.

8-9 hours Lodge
Day 11

Muktinath to Jomsom (2,770 m)

Descend through arid landscapes reminiscent of nearby Tibet to reach Jomsom. Overnight in a lodge.

~7 hours Lodge
Day 12

Drive from Jomsom to Pokhara

Return to Pokhara by vehicle (approximately 8-9 hours).

~8-9 hours Vehicle
