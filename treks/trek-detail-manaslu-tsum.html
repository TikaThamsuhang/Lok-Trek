Manaslu Trek via Tsum Valley

An Unforgettable Trekking Experience in Nepal

The Manaslu Trek via Tsum Valley is one of the most spectacular and unspoiled trekking adventures in Nepal, offering a unique immersion into Himalayan culture, nature, and spirituality. This exceptional trek passes through the remote Tsum Valley, renowned for its ancient monasteries, traditional villages, and warm-hearted local communities, while providing breathtaking views of the Manaslu massif and surrounding peaks.

Trekkers journey through rhododendron forests, crystal-clear rivers, and cross iconic high passes such as Larkya La Pass (5,160 m), all while discovering the daily life of local Himalayan communities. Ideal for lovers of authentic adventure in Nepal, the Manaslu Trek via Tsum Valley combines cultural discovery, panoramic photography, and a moderate to challenging physical experience, far from the classic tourist trails.

The best seasons for this trek are March to June and September to December, when weather conditions are most favorable. Prepare to explore the Himalayas in all their splendor and experience a journey that will stay with you forever through the Manaslu Trek and Tsum Valley Trek, one of Nepal's most sought-after trekking routes.

Optional visits to nearby monasteries and villages such as Mu Gompa and Rachen Gompa. Overnight at a lodge.

Lodge

Namrung - Samagaon (3,530 m)
