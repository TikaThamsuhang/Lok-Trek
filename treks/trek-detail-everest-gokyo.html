Everest Base Camp Trek via Gokyo

Gokyo Ri & Kala Patthar

The Everest Base Camp Trek via Gokyo is one of the most spectacular and diverse trekking routes in Nepal, offering a unique combination of glacial landscapes, turquoise lakes, and legendary Himalayan peaks. This itinerary passes through the Khumbu valleys, including the stunning Gokyo Lakes, and includes the ascent of Gokyo Ri (5,357 m) and the iconic Kala Patthar (5,545 m), providing breathtaking panoramic views of Mount Everest, Lhotse, Makalu, and Ama Dablam.

Duration 15 Days
Level Sustained
Group 2 - 12 Pers.
Max Alt. 5,545 m

Trekkers also experience traditional Sherpa villages, ancient Buddhist monasteries, and the rich local culture of the Khumbu region, while enjoying comfortable lodges and well-organized, safe trekking conditions. This trek requires good physical fitness and is an unforgettable experience for those seeking high-mountain adventure, cultural immersion, and incomparable Himalayan scenery.

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Kathmandu (1,350 m)

Arrival at Kathmandu International Airport, welcome and transfer to the hotel. The day is dedicated to rest, trek preparation, and a briefing with the local team.

0 km 0 m Hotel
Day 2

Flight Kathmandu/Ramechhap – Lukla (2,840 m) & Trek to Phakding (2,650 m)

Early morning flight to Lukla with spectacular Himalayan views. Start of the trek with an easy walk along the Dudh Koshi River, passing Sherpa villages and cultivated fields to reach Phakding. Overnight in a lodge.

~8 km +200 m / −400 m Lodge
Day 3

Phakding to Namche Bazaar (3,440 m)

The trail follows the valley, crossing several suspension bridges before a steady climb to Namche Bazaar, the capital of the Khumbu region and its main economic center. Overnight in a lodge.

~11 km +800 m Lodge
Day 4

Acclimatization Day in Namche Bazaar

Acclimatization hike to Khumjung village or Syangboche viewpoint, offering a first glimpse of Mount Everest. Return to Namche Bazaar for overnight stay.

~5 km +300 m / −300 m Lodge
Day 5

Namche Bazaar to Dole (4,110 m)

The trail leaves the classic Everest route and enters the Gokyo Valley. Gradual ascent through rhododendron forests and high-altitude pastures to Dole. Overnight in a lodge.

~11 km +670 m Lodge
Day 6

Dole to Machhermo (4,470 m)

The trek continues through a wider valley with impressive views of Cho Oyu. Natural acclimatization occurs due to the moderate ascent. Overnight in Machhermo.

~7 km +360 m Lodge
Day 7

Machhermo to Gokyo (4,790 m)

Gradual climb to the famous Gokyo Lakes, with their striking turquoise waters, dominated by Mount Cho Oyu. Arrival in Gokyo by early afternoon. Overnight in a lodge.

~7 km +320 m Lodge
Day 8

Ascent of Gokyo Ri (5,357 m) & Trek to Thangnak (4,700 m)

Early morning ascent of Gokyo Ri, one of the finest viewpoints in the Khumbu, offering exceptional views of Everest, Lhotse, Makalu, and Cho Oyu. After descending, continue with a scenic walk along the Ngozumpa Glacier, the longest glacier in Nepal, through a dramatic alpine landscape. Overnight in Thangnak.

~14 km +570 m / −570 m Lodge
Day 9

Thangnak – Cho La Pass (5,420 m) – Dzongla (4,830 m)

Steady ascent toward Cho La Pass, a high-altitude crossing that may involve snow or ice depending on the season. After crossing the pass, descend to Dzongla. Overnight in a lodge.

~8 km +720 m / −590 m Lodge
Day 10

Dzongla to Lobuche (4,940 m)

Traverse dramatic high-mountain terrain dominated by Nuptse and Lobuche Peak, rejoining the classic Everest Base Camp route. Overnight in Lobuche.

~6 km +200 m Lodge
Day 11

Lobuche – Gorak Shep (5,170 m) – Everest Base Camp (5,364 m)

Ascent to Gorak Shep, followed by a hike to Everest Base Camp, crossing the Khumbu Glacier moraine. Return to Gorak Shep for overnight stay.

~12 km +450 m Lodge
Day 12

Gorak Shep – Kala Patthar (5,545 m) – Pheriche (4,240 m)

Early morning climb to Kala Patthar, the most iconic viewpoint of Mount Everest. Afterward, a long descent to Pheriche. Overnight in a lodge.

~14 km +375 m / −1,300 m Lodge
Day 13

Pheriche to Namche Bazaar

Gradual descent through villages and forests back to Namche Bazaar. Overnight in a lodge.

~17 km −800 m Lodge
Day 14

Namche Bazaar to Lukla

Final trekking day along the Dudh Koshi River to Lukla. Overnight in a lodge.

~18 km −600 m Lodge
Day 15

Flight Lukla to Kathmandu

Morning flight back to Kathmandu.
