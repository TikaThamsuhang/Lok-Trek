Sacred Gosaikunda Lake & Langtang Valley Trek

Adventure and Spirituality

The Sacred Gosaikunda Lake and Langtang Valley Trek is an exceptional adventure in the Nepal Himalayas, combining high-mountain landscapes, traditional villages, and spiritual sites. This trek takes hikers through the Langtang Valley, renowned for its pine forests, lush green valleys, and Tamang villages, before reaching the sacred Gosaikunda Lake (4,380 m), an important Hindu and Buddhist pilgrimage site filled with spiritual significance.

Along the route, you will cross crystal-clear rivers, suspension bridges, and alpine meadows while discovering local culture, the warm hospitality of Tamang and Sherpa communities, and breathtaking panoramas of the Langtang, Ganesh Himal, and Dorje Lakpa peaks.

This trek requires good physical fitness and gradual altitude acclimatization, but rewards trekkers with an immersive experience blending adventure, untouched nature, and spirituality. With a well-organized and safe itinerary, the Sacred Gosaikunda Lake and Langtang Valley Trek is perfect for lovers of Himalayan trekking in Nepal, stunning mountain views, and cultural exploration.

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 1

Kathmandu to Syabrubesi (1,460 m)

Departure from Kathmandu to Syabrubesi by tourist bus. Enjoy panoramic mountain views on clear days. Approximately 6 hours' drive. Overnight at a lodge.

~6 hours Lodge
Day 2

Syabrubesi to Lama Hotel (2,470 m)

We follow the Langtang Khola, with most of the trail passing through forest. It is possible to see monkeys and wild honeybees on the cliffs. After crossing the Langtang Khola via a suspension bridge, a steep ascent brings us to Lama Hotel.

~7 hours Lodge
Day 3

Lama Hotel to Langtang Village (3,400 m)

A gradual ascent through forest, with the first views of Langtang Lirung (7,246 m). Upon reaching Ghora Tabela, yaks can often be seen grazing in the meadows. We pass traditional Tamang houses, mani stones, and prayer walls before arriving in Langtang.

~6 hours Lodge
Day 4

Langtang Village to Kyanjin Gompa (3,870 m)

A gentle climb through wide alpine meadows brings us to Kyanjin Gompa. Magnificent landscapes unfold with small villages at the foot of the Langtang Himal massif. A short afternoon walk below Kyanjin Gompa village is recommended.

~3.5 hours Lodge
Day 5

Ascent of Tsergo Ri (4,985 m)

Early departure around 6:00 a.m. for this major excursion. From the summit viewpoint, enjoy a spectacular panoramic view of Langtang Lirung (7,246 m), Naya Khang (5,846 m), Dorje Lakpa (6,990 m), Langshisha Ri (6,427 m), Gangchempo (6,378 m), and, in the distance, the world's 14th highest peak, Shishapangma (8,013 m). A demanding but rewarding day.

~8 hours 4,985 m Kyanjin Gompa
Day 6

Kyanjin Gompa to Lama Hotel (2,400 m)

Descent back to Lama Hotel following the same trail as the ascent.

~7 hours Lodge
Day 7

Lama Hotel to Thulo Syabru (2,470 m)

Trek to Thulo Syabru with superb views of Ganesh Himal along the route.

6-7 hours Lodge
Day 8

Thulo Syabru to Cholangpati (3,650 m)

Climb toward Cholangpati. There is a chance to spot the rare red panda along the trail.

5-6 hours Lodge
Day 9

Cholangpati to Gosaikunda Lake (4,380 m)

Arrival at the sacred Gosaikunda Lake. Views include Langtang Himal, Ganesh Himal, and even Annapurna on the western horizon. We also visit Saraswati Kunda and Bhairav Kunda, lakes formed by the waters of Gosaikunda. Overnight near the lake.

4,380 m Lodge
Day 10

Gosaikunda Lake to Ghopte (3,580 m) via Lauribina La Pass (4,610 m)

Crossing the high Lauribina La Pass into the Helambu region. The pass is marked by a stone stupa and numerous prayer flags.

~5 hours 4,610 m pass Lodge
Day 11

Ghopte to Kutumsang (2,470 m)

The trail passes through wide pastures with ruins of old shepherd huts. From the route, enjoy views of Gangchempo and Dorje Lakpa peaks. We pass via Kalo Bhanjyang.

Lodge
Day 12

Kutumsang to Chisopani (2,295 m)

From Chisopani, enjoy panoramic views of several mountain ranges, for which the area is well known.

5-6 hours Lodge
Day 13

Chisopani to Sundarijal - Kathmandu

Trek from Chisopani to Sundarijal (about 4 hours), then drive approximately 1 hour back to Kathmandu. Enter Shivapuri National Park along the way. End of the trek.

~4 hours ~1 hour Trip End
