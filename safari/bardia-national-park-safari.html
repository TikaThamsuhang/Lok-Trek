Bardia National Park, located in western Nepal, is the perfect destination for a wild and authentic safari experience, far from the crowds.

This overland journey from Kathmandu allows for a gradual immersion into the landscapes of the Terai region and offers one of the best chances to spot the Bengal tiger in Nepal.

Why Choose Bardia National Park?

Bardiya National Park is the largest lowland national park in Nepal. Less visited than Chitwan, it attracts travelers seeking a quiet, off-the-beaten-path safari in pristine wilderness.

Wildlife you may encounter:

  • Bengal tiger
  • One-horned rhinoceros
  • Wild elephants
  • Leopards, sloth bears
  • More than 400 bird species
