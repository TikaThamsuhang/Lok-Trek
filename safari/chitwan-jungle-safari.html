Chitwan National Park Safari

Discover Chitwan National Park, one of Nepal’s natural treasures and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

This trip from Kathmandu takes you into the heart of the tropical Terai jungle, where you will encounter exceptional wildlife and the traditional Tharu culture. An ideal itinerary to complement a trek or a cultural stay in Nepal.

Why Visit Chitwan National Park?

Located in southern Nepal, Chitwan National Park is famous for its remarkable biodiversity. It is home to the one-horned rhinoceros, the Bengal tiger, gharial crocodiles, and more than 500 species of birds. The park offers an authentic safari experience, combining wild nature, wildlife observation, and cultural encounters.

Kathmandu – Chitwan National Park

Early morning departure from Kathmandu towards Chitwan. Check in at lodge/eco-lodge in Sauraha. Afternoon Tharu village visit. Evening Tharu cultural dance performance and dinner.

6-8 hours drive Lodge
Day 2

Jungle Safari in Chitwan

Full day exploration. Jeep safari (4x4) to spot rhinos, tigers, etc. Canoeing on Rapti River. Jungle walk or optional activities.

Safari Activities Lodge
Day 3

Chitwan – Kathmandu or Pokhara
