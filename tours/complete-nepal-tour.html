Complete Nepal Tour

Culture, Nature, Adventure & Spirituality

This complete tour of Nepal offers a unique immersion combining gentle trekking, culture, nature, adventure, and spirituality. From discovering the UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Kathmandu, Patan, and Bhaktapur, to wildlife safaris in Chitwan National Park, and through the spectacular landscapes of Pokhara and the Himalayas, this journey provides a perfect balance between exploration and relaxation.

Sunrise and sunset views at Sarangkot and Nagarkot, encounters with Tharu culture, participation in local festivals (if one takes place during the stay), and a shamanic experience in Dolakha offer authentic and unforgettable moments. The program ends with meditation days, ideal for reconnecting with yourself in a peaceful environment.

This organized Nepal tour, accessible to everyone, is ideal for travelers wishing to discover Nepal in all its diversity—ancient traditions, wildlife, and Himalayan panoramas.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Sightseeing in Kathmandu

Full-day sightseeing in Kathmandu. Visit Swayambhunath Stupa, Patan Durbar Square, Boudhanath Stupa, and Pashupatinath Temple.

Kathmandu Valley Hotel
Day 2

Kathmandu to Chitwan National Park

Drive from Kathmandu to Chitwan National Park. In the evening, visit a Tharu village and enjoy a Tharu cultural program.

Tourist Bus Resort/Hotel
Day 3

Chitwan Jungle Safari Activity

After breakfast: canoeing, jungle walk, and visit to the elephant breeding center. After lunch: jeep safari or elephant safari in Chitwan National Park.

Wildlife Activities Resort/Hotel
Day 4

Drive to Pokhara

Drive to Pokhara. Depending on time and weather: evening boat ride on Fewa Lake (1 hour) or leisure time around Lakeside. Cultural programs are held every evening near the temple area.

Tourist Bus Fewa Lake Hotel
Day 5

Sarangkot Sunrise & Sightseeing in Pokhara

Early morning (around 5:00 AM) drive to Sarangkot to admire the Himalayan sunrise. Return to the hotel for breakfast and refreshment. Around 10:00 AM: Full-day sightseeing in Pokhara, including Davis Falls, Gupteshwor Mahadev Cave, International Mountain Museum, World Peace Pagoda, and the tallest Shiva temple.

Himalayan Views Hotel
Day 6

Pokhara to Kathmandu

Return drive from Pokhara to Kathmandu.

Tourist Bus Hotel
Day 7

Kathmandu to Nagarkot

Drive to Nagarkot, visiting Changunarayan Temple (one of the oldest temples in the Kathmandu Valley) on the way. Enjoy the sunset view from Nagarkot.

Culture & Views Hotel
Day 8

Nagarkot to Bhaktapur

Early morning sunrise view. After breakfast, return to Bhaktapur. Afternoon walking tour and sightseeing in Bhaktapur.

Ancient City Hotel
Day 9

Departure to Dolakha

Early morning departure to Dolakha for a shamanic experience. Approximately 8 hours by bus and 3 hours of walking to reach the shaman's village near the Tibetan border.

8 hours drive 3 hours walk Homestay/Lodge
Day 10

Return to Kathmandu

Return journey to Kathmandu.

Tourist Bus Hotel
Day 11

Meditation Day

Meditation day (on request). Ideal for reconnecting with yourself in a peaceful environment.

Wellness Hotel
Day 12

Festival Day (Reserved)

Reserved day to celebrate a local festival, if one occurs during the stay.
