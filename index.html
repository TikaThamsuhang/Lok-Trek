Discover Nepal differently with Lok Treks Nepal, a local trekking agency in Nepal specializing in organizing treks and adventure trips in the heart of the Himalayas.

We guide travelers along the most beautiful routes in the country—from the legendary trails of Everest and Annapurna to more remote and less crowded regions such as Manaslu, Langtang, Tsum Valley, and Helambu.

Along the trails, you will pass through traditional villages, rhododendron forests, terraced rice fields, and breathtaking high mountain passes, with spectacular views of the world’s highest peaks. Choosing Lok Treks Nepal means experiencing more than just a trek—it’s a true human adventure, deeply connected to the soul of Nepal.
