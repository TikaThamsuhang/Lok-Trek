The Founder

Welcome to the Mountains of Nepal!

My name is Lok, a professional mountain guide and trekking organizer, deeply passionate about the majestic peaks and extraordinary landscapes of my homeland.

After completing my studies, I began my trekking career in 2020, combining my in-depth knowledge of the terrain with a lifelong love for the Himalayas.

Our Legacy

A Proud Family Tradition

I am proud to carry on a family tradition. My father, Moti, is an experienced porter with over 25 years of experience. He has dedicated his life to guiding travelers through the mountains, carrying their loads with commitment, respect, and professionalism.

"His journey and values deeply inspired me to become a French-speaking guide and to offer authentic, human-centered travel experiences."
— Lok

Our Promise to You

Today, I organize and personally guide treks along the most beautiful routes in Nepal—from lush green valleys to the snow-covered peaks—offering tailor-made experiences adapted to each traveler’s needs.

Whether you are an experienced adventurer or a curious traveler, I would be delighted to guide you in discovering the natural and cultural wonders of the Himalayas. My goal is to provide you with an unforgettable experience, ensuring safety, comfort, and respect for the mountains and local communities.
