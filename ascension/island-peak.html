Island Peak (Imja Tse)

High-Altitude Trek & Climbing Expedition

Island Peak (Imja Tse, 6,189 m) is one of the most popular trekking peaks in Nepal, ideal for trekkers and climbers seeking a true high-mountain experience with a moderately technical ascent. Compared to Mera Peak, Island Peak is more challenging, involving glacier travel, steeper slopes, and the use of crampons, ice axe, and fixed ropes. It is best suited for trekkers with good high-altitude experience and basic mountaineering skills.

Duration 20 Days
Level Challenging
Group 2 - 8 Pers.
Summit 6,189 m

Day by Day Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Kathmandu (1,350 m)

Arrival at Kathmandu airport, welcome and transfer to the hotel in Thamel. Rest day, trek preparation, and briefing with the local team.

Hotel
Day 2

Flight Kathmandu - Lukla (2,840 m) & Trek to Phakding (2,650 m)

Early morning flight to Lukla, gateway to the Khumbu region. Easy trek along the Dudh Koshi River through Sherpa villages to Phakding.

Flight 3-4 hours Lodge
Day 3

Phakding - Namche Bazaar (3,440 m)

Trek through pine forests and suspension bridges, entering Sagarmatha National Park. Steep ascent to Namche Bazaar, the commercial hub of Khumbu.

4-5 hours +900 m Lodge
Day 4

Acclimatization Day in Namche Bazaar

Acclimatization hike to Khumjung and Khunde villages or Syangboche viewpoint. Return to Namche for the night.

4-5 hours Lodge
Day 5

Namche Bazaar - Thame (3,800 m)

Leaving the main Everest trail, the trek follows the Bhote Koshi Valley to Thame, a traditional Sherpa village with an important monastery.

4-5 hours Lodge
Day 6

Thame - Lungden (4,380 m)

Trek through a wild and rugged valley with gradual ascent, ideal for acclimatization. Arrival at Lungden, the last village before Renjo La Pass.

4-5 hours Lodge
Day 7

Lungden - Renjo La Pass (5,360 m) - Gokyo (4,790 m)

Early ascent to Renjo La Pass with spectacular views of Everest, Lhotse, and the Gokyo Lakes. Descent to Gokyo village.

5-6 hours 5,360 m pass Lodge
Day 8

Gokyo Ri (5,357 m) & Gokyo Lakes

Optional early morning climb of Gokyo Ri for panoramic views of Everest, Cho Oyu, and Makalu. Rest and acclimatization in the afternoon.

~4 hours Lodge
Day 9

Gokyo - Thangnak (4,700 m)

Short walk along the Ngozumpa Glacier, the longest glacier in Nepal, preparing for Cho La Pass.

2 hours Lodge
Day 10

Thangnak - Cho La Pass (5,420 m) - Dzongla (4,830 m)

Steep ascent to Cho La Pass, sometimes requiring hands depending on conditions. Long descent to Dzongla.

5-6 hours 5,420 m pass Lodge
Day 11

Dzongla - Lobuche (4,940 m)

Traverse high-altitude landscapes dominated by Nuptse and Lobuche Peak.

2-3 hours Lodge
Day 12

Lobuche - Gorak Shep (5,170 m) - Everest Base Camp (5,364 m)

Gradual ascent to Gorak Shep, then hike to Everest Base Camp across the Khumbu Glacier moraine. Return to Gorak Shep.

7 hours Lodge
Day 13

Gorak Shep - Kala Patthar (5,545 m) - Lobuche

Early morning climb to Kala Patthar for sunrise views of Mount Everest, then descent to Lobuche.

5-6 hours 5,545 m Lodge
Day 14

Lobuche - Kongma La Pass (5,535 m) - Chhukung (4,730 m)

Cross the most remote and demanding of the Three High Passes, Kongma La. Descend to Chhukung.

6-7 hours 5,535 m pass Lodge
Day 15

Chhukung - Island Peak Base Camp (5,200 m)

Steep ascent through moraines and glacial terrain to Island Peak Base Camp. Camp setup and preparation for summit.

4-5 hours Tent
Day 16

Summit Island Peak (6,189 m) - Return to Chhukung (4,730 m)

Early morning climb over rocky terrain, glacier travel with rope, and a final steep snow/ice slope (40-45°) using fixed ropes. After reaching the summit, descend to Chhukung.

9-11 hours Summit 6,189 m Lodge
Day 17

Chhukung - Tengboche (3,885 m)

Descent through the Imja Khola Valley with stunning views of Ama Dablam. Overnight near Tengboche / Pangboche.

5-6 hours Lodge
Day 18

Tengboche - Namche Bazaar

Return to Namche through rhododendron forests and Sherpa villages.

5-6 hours Lodge
Day 19

Namche Bazaar - Lukla

Final trekking day back to Lukla. End of the Khumbu adventure.

4-5 hours Lodge
Day 20

Flight Lukla - Kathmandu

Morning flight back to Kathmandu and transfer to hotel. Free time in the city.

Flight Trip End
